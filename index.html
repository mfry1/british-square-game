British Square

A Classic Board Game

Current Player: Player 1

Place your piece on any empty square

Player 1 Pieces: 0
Player 2 Pieces: 0

British Square Rules (1978):

  • Goal: Place the most pieces on the 5x5 board
  • Placement: Click any empty square to place your piece
  • Key Rule: Cannot place next to opponent pieces (up/down/left/right)
  • Diagonal: You CAN place diagonally next to opponents
  • Special: Player 1 cannot use center square on first turn
  • End: Game ends when both players cannot move
  • Winner: Player with the most pieces wins!
